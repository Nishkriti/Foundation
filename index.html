The Animal Feeding Project


                            Nishkriti Foundation is a nonprofit animal welfare organization based in Bangladesh, a country ridden with suffering animals on the streets, fighting disease, poor hygiene and starvation. With the goal of helping as many animals on the streets as possible, our mission is to raise awareness about the difficult lives of cats and dogs on the streets, get people to see and learn about their heart-breaking state of living, and chime in to help these animals find shelter, food and other necessities. We plan to use charity donations from fundraisers and livestreams (in the future) to run animal welfare projects throughout our journey, from feeding to helping adopt from the streets, with a final destination of opening a fully standardized animal shelter.

                            This dream can only be achieved through the help of people’s kindness and generosity as our very first project is to feed a number of cats and dogs in one region on one street regularly funded by money raised through a fundraiser that will remain open the entire month before. At the end of the month, we will reveal how much money was raised and how many animals it can feed. Then we will decide, using the survey on the post, to decide which region to start our project. So please help us out by filling out the survey to tell us what area you think will benefit most from our project. Then we will use the money to feed as many of cats and dogs in a certain region as we can afford through it, twice a day, for the entire next month. All of the feeding will be livestreamed for people to watch and donate to and the fundraisers will reopen for next month’s feeding. This will be repeated every month and the same animals on the same area will be fed and featured on the streams. The higher the donation money reaches at the end of month, the more we will be able to add a new animal to the feeding roster. All the animals on the streams will be up for adoption and assistance with adoption process and information on medical support such as vaccination and veterinary offices will be offered. So if the money is higher than last month by enough to feed another animal, we will feed one more animal. Over time as this project grows with increasing number of animals we can feed, we will expand the regions where we can feed. Any extra money that is not enough to feed a new animal for the month but still beyond the bar will either be stored for future projects in a separate fundraiser or spent on advertising the fundraiser itself, all done visible to the audience.

                            As this project grows, with enough support, more amazing projects to help these animals will be revealed. We plan to keep running projects like this until every cat and dog on the streets of Bangladesh is either adopted or fostered but this is only possible if we get support from an audience large enough to fund all our plans to help these helpless animals to a better life. So share this post with your friends and family, fill in the survey and follow the page to be notified when a project begins and help out if you can. Remember, anything you can give means a LOT to a hungry little cat or dog sniffing around for food near trashcans and roadsides.
